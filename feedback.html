Course Feedback

The way this course was thought was backwards to what should be done. Every coding course starts off with an explanation of what an IDE. Even if the course is about HTML.

Forced Feedback

>There are people who have experience and wish to never log into git-hub again. There are people who have never experienced coding and do not want to experience it. There are people who have never experienced coding and wish to know more, however the chaotic nature of this study and course have pushed them away from coding and exploring their abilities. Forcing people into giving feedback so they can pass their own assignment, only provides an environment where people give less than useful feedback and creates detremental situations to people who actually need and want good-constructive feedback.

Self Study

Self studying is the best way to learn coding, I agree on that. However, most people in this course have never even seen code, throwing them under the bus like that with no previous warning creates questions of the legitamacy of their investment in this study
